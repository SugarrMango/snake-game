Snake game

Rules

Objective: -Try to make the snake as long as possible. You can do that by moving the snake to the fruits so that the snake is eating the fruit.

Controls: -Arrow up = up -Arrow down = down -Arrow left - left -Arrow right = right

Eliminations: -Snake touches wall -Snakes head touches snakes body

Score: -Whenever the snake eats a fruit the score increases by 1

Timer: -The timer shows how long your snake has been alive for

Pause: -By pressing ESC you can pause the game
